# UrbanLinkAI

UrbanLinkAI is an AI-powered compatibility and identity intelligence engine designed for modern dating platforms.

Dating apps today rely on shallow signals: swipes, photos, short bios, and limited prompts. UrbanLinkAI replaces this with deep, continuously evolving compatibility modeling based on who people actually are, not how they market themselves.

UrbanLinkAI is built to be licensed or acquired by major dating platforms such as Match Group, Tinder, Hinge, OkCupid, and future relationship-based social platforms.

---

## What UrbanLinkAI Does

UrbanLinkAI analyzes how people communicate, express emotion, reveal values, and respond to connection over time. It builds a living compatibility profile for each user that improves with every interaction.

Instead of matching users based on surface traits, UrbanLinkAI matches based on:

- Emotional tone
- Attachment style
- Communication patterns
- Conflict response
- Desire for intimacy vs independence
- Long-term vs short-term relationship orientation
- Trust, vulnerability, and honesty markers

These signals cannot be faked, because they come from real behavior, not profiles.
