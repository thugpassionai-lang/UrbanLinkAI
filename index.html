What UrbanLinkAI Is

UrbanLinkAI is an AI-powered compatibility and identity intelligence engine designed to be embedded inside modern dating platforms.

Instead of relying on swipes, bios, and photos, UrbanLinkAI analyzes how people actually communicate, connect, and behave over time.

  • Conflict response

  • Long-term vs short-term orientation
  • Trust and vulnerability signals
